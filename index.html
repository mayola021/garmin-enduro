Why You Should Keep Your Garmin Enduro Updated

Your Garmin Enduro is a powerhouse of endurance tracking, multisport metrics, GPS navigation, solar charging and long-battery life. To ensure it runs smoothly—whether on rugged trails, steep climbs, ultramarathons or everyday training—you should install every firmware update.

  • Performance Enhancements: Each update typically addresses glitches like GPS drops, sensor pairing issues, firmware lag, and button response delays.
  • New Features: Garmin steadily adds improvements—advanced fitness tracking, new widget pages, solar battery optimizations, and updated UI elements.
  • Security & Stability: Firmware updates include fixes to known vulnerabilities and increase overall device stability.
  • Accessory Support: Updates add compatibility with new sensor bands (heart rate, cadence, power meters), earbuds or Galileo satellite systems.

Preparing For The Update

Before you click “Update Now,” make sure you follow these preparatory steps:

  • Fully charge your Enduro to at least 50% (ideally 100%), or plug in during the update.
  • Backup your data using Garmin Connect or Garmin Express—this includes activity history, settings, custom workouts, and courses.
  • Update Garmin Connect mobile app or Garmin Express desktop app to the latest version.
  • Use a stable internet connection during update download to prevent corruption.
  • Wear your watch and keep it awake during mobile updates; avoid low-power mode.

Step-by-Step Update Process

Using Garmin Connect App (Mobile)

  1. Open the Garmin Connect app on your smartphone.
  2. Ensure your Enduro is paired via Bluetooth and near your phone.
  3. Tap the “More” menu (☰ or •••) and go to “Garmin Devices”.
  4. Select “Garmin Enduro”. If a new update is available, a prompt appears.
  5. Tap “Install” or “Download Update”, agree to terms, and allow Bluetooth installation.
  6. Keep your watch close and charged. Once completed, it will reboot and apply changes.

Using Garmin Express (Desktop Windows/Mac)

  1. Launch Garmin Express and plug your Enduro into USB.
  2. Select your watch and wait for it to sync.
  3. If a firmware update shows, click “Install” or “Install Update”.
  4. Wait for download and installation to complete. Do not disconnect until fully done.
  5. When installation finishes, Garmin Express may ask to “Eject” the device.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Here are solutions to problems you might encounter:

  • Update fails to start: Ensure Garmin Connect or Express is fully updated; restart the app and device and retry.
  • Installation hangs at 99%: Reboot your Enduro by holding the light button for 15 seconds. Retry installation.
  • Bluetooth connection drops: Try updating via Garmin Express over USB instead, or ensure phone is nearby.
  • Strange watch behavior after update (UI slow, missing features): Perform a soft reset—hold back‑lap‑light buttons until watch reboots.
